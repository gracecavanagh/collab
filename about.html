About This Project

3D Utility & Infrastructure Planning at Ontario Parks

Ontario Parks, a division of the Ministry of the Environment, Conservation and Parks in Ontario, Canada, safeguards numerrous natural and cultural resources across parks and protected lands, while also facilitating recreational and educational activities. Such Provincial parks has uniques facilities and resources. Our projects intends to model these assets to improve utilites infrastructure maintenance, demonstrating the benefit of digitized resources for all parks. We want to successfully engange park management and maintenance team by providing interactive representations of parks resources
